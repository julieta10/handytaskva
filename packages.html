At HandyTask VA, we offer three types of specialized virtual assistants: administrative, marketing, and specialist VAs. Our Marketing VAs are adept at handling various marketing duties, including content creation, social media management, email marketing, copywriting, ebook development, market research, basic graphic design, and basic video and audio editing. Lastly, our Specialist VAs cater to technical and specialized tasks such as website development and design, advanced graphic design and branding, advanced audio and video editing, podcast editing and management, search engine optimization, automation, ads, and virtual production.
